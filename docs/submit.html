What is Hackerboard?

A community-curated link board for hackers and builders. Every post is a GitHub Discussion — no algorithm, no tracking, no walled garden. The site rebuilds from those discussions every 15 minutes.

What to submit

  • Articles, tools, or libraries worth reading
  • Show HB: your own projects and experiments
  • Releases, papers, or deep-dives on programming and systems

How

  • Click the button below — a GitHub Discussion template opens
  • Fill in title, link, description, and tags
  • Submit — your post appears within 15 minutes

Guidelines

  • Link to the original source, not aggregators
  • Use Show HB: when submitting your own work
  • Spam or off-topic posts may be removed
