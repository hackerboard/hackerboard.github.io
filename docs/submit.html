Submit to Hackerboard

What is Hackerboard?

Hackerboard is a community-curated link board for hackers, builders, and curious minds. Every submission lives as a GitHub Discussion — no proprietary account wall, no black-box algorithm, no tracking. The board is rebuilt from those discussions every 15 minutes and published as a static site.

What to submit

  • Technical articles, tools, or libraries worth reading
  • Show HB: your own projects, experiments, or side-hacks
  • Releases, papers, talks, or deep-dives on programming, systems, or ideas
  • Anything a thoughtful hacker would find genuinely interesting

How to submit

  • Click the button below — it opens a pre-filled GitHub Discussion template
  • Fill in: title, link, a short description, and relevant tags
  • Submit the discussion — your post will appear on the board within 15 minutes

Guidelines

  • Link to the original source, not to aggregators or paywall mirrors
  • Use the Show HB: prefix when submitting your own work
  • Keep tags short and specific (e.g. rust, ai, tools, release)
  • Off-topic, spam, or low-effort submissions may be removed
